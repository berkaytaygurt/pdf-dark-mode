Why Use Dark Mode for PDFs?

Reading PDF documents on bright screens can cause severe eye strain, especially at night. Most PDF readers do not offer a native dark mode. Our free online tool inverts the colors of your PDF files, turning glaring white backgrounds into soothing dark colors without modifying the original text.

Features of Our PDF Inverter

1. Privacy First: All processing is done locally in your browser. We do not upload your sensitive documents to our servers.
2. Multiple Filters: Choose between Pure Dark, Soft Gray, or Sepia based on your lighting environment.
3. Multi-File Support: Convert multiple files simultaneously to save time.
